## Recommended Additions (Standard P&L Practice):

### 1. Executive Summary / Key Highlights ⭐ HIGH PRIORITY
- Total Revenue
- Gross Profit & Margin
- Net Profit & Margin
- Key insights (e.g., "Revenue increased 15% vs last period")

### 2. Revenue Breakdown by Channel ⭐ HIGH PRIORITY
- Direct Sales (Walk-in/MyShop)
- Booking Sales
- Consignment Revenue
- Percentage breakdown
- **Data Available**: `_salesByChannel` list

### 3. Expense Breakdown ⭐ MEDIUM PRIORITY
- COGS details (if available)
- Operating Expenses by category
- Major expense items
- **Data Available**: Expenses table with categories

### 4. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) ⭐ MEDIUM PRIORITY
- Gross Profit Margin %
- Net Profit Margin %
- Operating Profit Margin %
- Revenue Growth Rate (if period comparison available)
- Average Daily Sales

### 5. Notes & Disclaimers ⭐ LOW PRIORITY
- Calculation methodology
- Data accuracy disclaimer
- "This report is generated automatically"
- "For accounting purposes, please consult with accountant"

### 6. Period Comparison (Optional) ⭐ LOW PRIORITY
- Current vs Previous Period
- Month-over-Month (MoM) changes
- Year-over-Year (YoY) changes
- **Requires**: Previous period data calculation
